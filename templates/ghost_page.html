Ghost game

Rules:

All the players will be shown a word, except for one - the ghost.

Starting with a particular player, each describes the word they saw. The ghost must try to fit in with the others.

After a fixed number of rounds, or time (your choice), all players must vote for who they think ghost is.

If the players fail to choose the ghost, they lose.

If they succeed, the ghost has one more chance to win - if they guess the word that the other players saw correctly.

To play:

When you are ready to start a new game, press the button below.

Your word will appear below the button.
