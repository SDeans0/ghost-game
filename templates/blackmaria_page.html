Black Maria

Rules:

Black Maria is a trick taking game with no trumps. Hearts won in any trick are penalty points (face value, 10 for royalty, 15 for the Ace), as is the Queen of Spades (50). A third of the deck is dealt to each player (minus the 2 of clubs in 3P mode). The person who opened the room begins the game. After that, the winner of the trick leads.

To play:

When you are ready to start a new game, press one of the buttons below.

Your hand will appear at the bottom of the screen. Click on a card to play it.
